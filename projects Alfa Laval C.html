Alfa Laval, Kunshan (2017-2018)

  • Construction Date: 2018
  • Location: Kunshan, China
  • Surface Area: 18,000 m2
  • Contracting Investor: Alfa Laval
  • Category: Production Facility

Alfa Laval AB is a Swedish company with a history since 1883. The company deals in the production of specialized products and solutions for heavy industry. The products are used to heat, cool, separate and transport such products as oil, water, chemicals, beverages, foodstuffs, starch and pharmaceuticals.

In late 2016 Alfa Laval decided to build a new plant in Kunshan with around 18,000 m2 of workshop, offices, canteen and other social areas.

As a long term consultant of Alfa Laval in China, Bencer was awarded the Project Management services for the construction of this new plant.

Reference Contact: www.alfalaval.com
